Let's Explore Measurement! 📏

Interactive Mathematics Portal for Year 4

Task 1: Measuring with your Feet 👟

In the past, people used their own feet to measure lengths! Find 4 different paths around you. First, guess (estimate) how many foot-steps it will take, then walk it out and count them!

Task 2: Make & Use Your Own Ruler 📐

Follow your teacher's instructions to trace and cut out your custom foot-ruler, folding it into 8 equal 'units' or 'hops'. Give your unique unit a fun name, then estimate and measure 5 objects in the room!

Task 3: Is a 'Metre' Best? 🏃‍♂️

A metre is the basic unit for measuring length in the metric system. It is about one long stride. Choose YES if a metre ruler is the best tool to measure these, or NO if it is too small or too big.

Task 4: Choosing the Perfect Metric Unit 🗺️

Pick the absolute best metric unit for each item: mm (tiny), cm (small), m (medium/human scale), or km (very long distances).
